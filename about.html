We Started as Contractors Ourselves.

iGroup was born from frustration. Our founders spent decades contracting across engineering, IT, and finance — and saw the same problems everywhere. Contractors negotiating blind. Compliance eating into productive hours. Careers drifting without strategy.

We asked a simple question: why don't contractors have access to the same intelligence and support that Fortune 500 companies give their permanent workforce?

So we built it. An AI-powered platform that analyses market rates in real time, manages compliance automatically, and provides genuine career intelligence. Not another umbrella. Not another agency. A platform that works exclusively for you.

From Idea to Intelligence Platform

A timeline of ambition, research, and relentless focus on what contractors actually need.

The Idea

A group of contractors asked: why are we negotiating blind? Why is compliance such a burden? Why doesn't anyone build tools that actually work for us?

The Research

Two years analysing rate data across 15+ sectors and 200+ geographies. Understanding what contractors need, not what the industry wanted to sell them.

The Platform

AI-powered rate intelligence engine goes live. For the first time, contractors can see exactly what they should be earning — down to their postcode.

The Network

500+ contractors joined. £12M+ in rate uplifts identified. A community of professionals who negotiate from strength, not guesswork.

The Future

Global expansion. Full contractor portal. The standard for professional contracting. We're building what the industry should have built decades ago.
