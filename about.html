Our Story

We Started as Contractors Ourselves.

iGroup was founded by professionals who spent years working as independent contractors across sectors from technology to engineering to financial services. We negotiated blind, overpaid for insurance, and navigated IR35 without guidance. We watched talented people leave money on the table — every single assignment.

So we built the platform we always wished existed. One that treats contractors like the skilled professionals they are — not as commodities to be placed and forgotten. One backed by real data, real expertise, and real care for long-term career outcomes.

Today, iGroup serves contractors across 15+ sectors and 4 global offices. But our purpose hasn't changed: give every contractor the intelligence and support that Fortune 500 companies give their permanent workforce.

"Every contractor deserves the same intelligence and support that Fortune 500 companies give their permanent workforce."

— The iGroup Mission
