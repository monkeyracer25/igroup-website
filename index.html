Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is iGroup and how does it support professional contractors?

iGroup is a management consultancy exclusively serving professional contractors. We provide AI-powered rate intelligence, IR35 compliance management, career strategy, and contract review services — all designed to help you manage, optimise, and protect your contracting career.

How quickly can I get started with iGroup?

Most contractors are fully onboarded within 48 hours. We begin with a comprehensive assessment of your current situation, then build your personalised compliance framework and connect you to our intelligence platform.

What makes iGroup different from umbrella companies?

We're not an umbrella company. iGroup is a specialist management consultancy. We don't process your payroll — we provide the intelligence, compliance management, and career support that empowers you to make better decisions about your contracting career.

How does iGroup's rate intelligence work?

Our proprietary AI analyses market data across all major sectors, providing real-time benchmarking, negotiation insights, and trend analysis. This gives you the data to negotiate from a position of strength.

Does iGroup support contractors working internationally?

Yes. We support contractors across multiple jurisdictions with cross-border compliance, international tax guidance, and global mobility support. Wherever your career takes you, iGroup is with you.
