Chapter 11: What Joint & Several Liability Means for You

The introduction of joint and several liability changes everything for umbrella workers and contractors. This landmark legislative change means that end clients, agencies, and umbrella companies now share responsibility for ensuring correct tax deductions. If your umbrella company fails to pay the right amount of tax, HMRC can pursue the entire supply chain — including the end client. For contractors, this means increased due diligence requirements, more scrutiny of umbrella arrangements, and a fundamental shift in how compliant supply chains are structured. We break down exactly what this means for your working arrangements and what steps you should be taking right now to protect yourself.
