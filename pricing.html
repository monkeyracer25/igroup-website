Pricing Questions

Can I change plans?

Yes, upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect at the next billing date.

Is there a contract?

No. Month-to-month billing. Cancel anytime with no penalties.

What is the £250 welcome bonus?

New Professional members receive £250 in rate optimisation credits, equivalent to an expert rate review.

Is VAT included?

Prices shown exclude VAT. UK contractors will be charged VAT at the prevailing rate.

Can I expense the membership?

Yes. iGroup membership is a legitimate business expense for limited company contractors.

What if I'm between assignments?

We offer a pause feature. Freeze your subscription for up to 3 months without losing your data or history.
