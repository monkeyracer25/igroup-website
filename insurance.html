FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Questions About Insurance

Everything you need to know about accessing group insurance through iGroup. If your question is not answered here, our team is always happy to help.

Do I need all six types of cover?

No. Most contractors start with Professional Indemnity and Public Liability, then add cover as their needs grow. Our advisors can help you identify exactly what is required based on your sector and client contracts. There is no pressure to take more than you need.

Can I keep my existing policies?

Yes. We can review your current cover and only replace policies where we can offer better terms. Many contractors find that switching even one or two policies to our group rates delivers significant annual savings while maintaining or improving their level of protection.

How do claims work?

You contact our team directly. We manage the entire claims process with the insurer on your behalf. From initial notification through to settlement, our dedicated claims team handles all communications and paperwork so you can focus on your work.

Are pre-existing conditions covered?

For health insurance, pre-existing conditions are reviewed on a case-by-case basis. We work to find the best terms available. Our group policies often provide more favourable terms for pre-existing conditions than individual policies on the open market.

Can I add my family?

Yes. Private health and life insurance can be extended to cover partners and dependents at group rates. Family cover through iGroup is significantly more affordable than purchasing individual family policies, and our team can help you find the right level of cover for everyone.
