05 / FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions

What is iGroup and how does it support professional contractors?

iGroup is a professional contractor management consultancy. We provide IR35 compliance management, proprietary rate intelligence, and strategic career support — built exclusively for contractors who want to optimise their careers and earnings.

How quickly can I get started with iGroup?

Getting started is straightforward. After an initial consultation, most contractors are fully onboarded within 48 hours and have immediate access to our rate intelligence platform and dedicated compliance team.

What makes iGroup different from umbrella companies?

Unlike umbrella companies, iGroup is a management consultancy — not a payroll vehicle. We work exclusively for the contractor, providing strategic advice, compliance assurance, and market intelligence. Our interests are fully aligned with yours.

How does iGroup's rate intelligence work?

Our proprietary platform aggregates live market data across 15+ sectors and geographies. Combined with analyst insights and live contract data, we provide contractors with the benchmarks and negotiation intelligence they need to command the right rate.

Does iGroup support contractors working internationally?

Yes. iGroup supports contractors operating across multiple jurisdictions. Our global mobility team handles cross-border compliance, tax treaty analysis, and international rate benchmarking — ensuring you remain compliant and competitive wherever you work.
